For many, violence TV is directly related to the sharp increase of cruelty in our community. For others it has no impact on human behaviour. This is a controversial issue and both sides need to be presented.   

Those who are in favour of violence in entertainment, base their arguments on the fact that it cannot influence our society. It is true that most people have the advantage of separating fiction and reality. Therefore, not only do they avoid copying negative behaviours, but they also realize the significance of dealing with problems peacefully. Moreover, in todays civilized world it is everyone’s right to entertain themselves according to their preferences, so a possible ban of such programs would be a violation to personal freedom as far as the choice of entertainment goes. Even though young children and adolescents may be more influenced than adults, there are indications about the suitability of tv programs and it is the parent’s responsibility to monitor their children’s TV history and act accordingly.
