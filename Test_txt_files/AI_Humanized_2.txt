When we look at the future of our nation my fellow Americans it is easy to feel scared. We read the news. It is not good. We go to the store. Things cost a lot of money. We see the weather changing companies failing and our communities falling apart. It is not hard to see the problems we are facing and think that our best days are behind us.

I am not here to talk about what is going wrong. I am here to talk about what we're going to do to make things better. The future is something we are building now step by step.

Imagine what our nation will be like ten years from now. Imagine a nation where if someone gets sick it does not mean their family will struggle to pay the bills. We will make energy from the wind that blows across our country and the sun that shines on our homes. This will create a lot of jobs that pay well. Our kids will be, in school reading and learning not practicing what to do in case of an emergency. They will be thinking about all the things they can do when they grow up.

This is not a dream.
